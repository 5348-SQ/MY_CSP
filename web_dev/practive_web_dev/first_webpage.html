Wait... Whats mech?

Well Im so glad you asked. Mechs, which also go by many names so numerus I can't be bothered to list. There has been much debate over the exact definiton of this term. In my circle, which contains only me (currently), defines a Mech as "A huminoid machine, ussally made for military applications, that contains a human pilot who controles the machine"; although gut feeling and other complications may overide this definiton on a case to case basis.

What do you mean by "Real Mechs"?

The two primary catagories of mech most mecha-lovers use are Real and Super. Super mechs are mechs that act more like a superhero rather then a pice of military hardware. Some good examples of this are the giant robots in Power Rangers and early japenese cartoons. "Real" mechs are mechs that think of them selves as pices of military hardware rather then super heros. Some examples of this style include the Mobile Suits from the first half of the One Year War in Mobile Suit Gundam and the Verticle Tanks from "Steel Batlion". I care primarly about these rather then super, becuse I simply find them more intrsting.
